Humming Garden Villas - Bhoodigere Cross

Humming Garden Villas

Humming Garden Villas is an upcoming luxury residential development located at Bhoodigere Cross, offering spacious 4 BHK villas that redefine elegant living. Set in a serene and green environment, these villas are designed with private gardens, large balconies, and smart home automation.

The community emphasizes sustainability with eco-friendly construction, solar panels, and rainwater harvesting systems. Residents will enjoy world-class amenities including a swimming pool, clubhouse, gym, jogging track, yoga lawn, and children's play area. With excellent connectivity to Devanahalli and Kempegowda International Airport just 20 km away, the project ensures effortless access to key areas.

The location is well-supported by quality infrastructure, reputed educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and retail centers. Humming Garden Villas also offers a secure living environment with 24/7 security, CCTV surveillance, gated access, and on-site property management. The roads within the community are wide and well-lit, enhancing safety and aesthetic appeal.

New Residential Project - Near Manyata Tech Park

New Residential Project

Located just 2 km from Manyata Tech Park, this upcoming residential project is a prime choice for urban professionals and families seeking a blend of luxury, convenience, and connectivity. The project is designed with open layouts, ample natural lighting, and modern finishes that cater to contemporary lifestyles.

Planned with urban sensibilities, it features a rooftop gym, co-working lounge, amphitheater, children’s play area, landscaped gardens, and an infinity-edge swimming pool. The apartments come equipped with high-speed internet, smart security systems, and energy-efficient fixtures. With seamless access to Hebbal (5 km), Outer Ring Road, and major IT hubs, daily commuting becomes stress-free.

The project also includes community-centric facilities such as a multipurpose hall, reading lounge, mini theater, and convenience store within the premises. A dedicated visitor parking area and EV charging stations further enhance modern living convenience.
